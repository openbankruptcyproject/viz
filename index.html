Methodology overview

Source: 220,037 federal bankruptcy cases harvested from PACER (2008-2026). Court codes follow PACER conventions (e.g., flsbk = U.S. Bankruptcy Court, Southern District of Florida; mowbk = U.S. Bankruptcy Court, Western District of Missouri). Dispositions are administrative classifications, not legal holdings; researchers seeking case-level legal reasoning should pull underlying dockets.

Per-dataset methodology, sample sizes, and caveats are documented on each dataset page. Source code: obp-empirical-toolkit.
